pub fn set_enabled(&mut self, enabled: bool, access: impl ConfigRegionAccess)

Enable MSI-X on the specified device feature.

Unlike with MSI, the MSI message data and delivery address is not contained within the capability, but instead in system memory, and pointed to by the BAR specified by MsixCapability::table_bar and MsixCapability::table_offset. The caller is therefore responsible for configuring this separately, as this crate does not have access to arbitrary physical memory.

Enable/disable masking of all interrupts for this PCI function.

Individual interrupt sources can be masked using mask field of the corresponding entry in the MSI-X table.
